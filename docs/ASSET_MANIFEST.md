# Kurogami UE5 Asset License Manifest

Every third-party or Fab/Megascans asset used in the Brickell showpiece (or any later region) gets
one row here before it is used in the project, not after. This is the license paper trail; keep it
current as assets are added.

**The boundary this manifest exists to enforce:** watches, jets, and brand set-dressing stay
original designs or clear homages under the Kurogami brand, never trademarked reproductions of a
real product (no genuine Audemars Piguet Royal Oak, no specific Bombardier jet model, no real brand
logos on containers or signage). Photoreal rendering makes passing off a real product accidental and
easy, so this line gets held harder here than it was in the web build, not softer. Fab/Megascans
architecture, materials, props, and vegetation are the safe, pre-licensed default; anything outside
that (a purchased Marketplace pack, a custom commission, a scanned real-world object) gets logged
here with enough detail that a legal review does not have to guess.
